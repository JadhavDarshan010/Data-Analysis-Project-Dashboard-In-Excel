# Sales Data Analysis Projet In MS - Excel
## Project Objective
- The main objective of this project is to analyze the company’s yearly sales performance for 2021 using MS Excel.
- The project aims to understand product-wise, region-wise, and salesperson-wise performance through a visual dashboard.
- It helps identify top-performing products, months, and salesmen, as well as low-performing areas that need improvement.
- The goal is to turn raw sales data into meaningful insights that support better business decisions and future sales planning.

## Thing's To look For -
1. Total  Revenue and Total Product Sales (2021 ).
2. Sales By Product , Sales By Month & Sales By Region.
3. Product Wise Sales (Create A Chart ) , Top 3 & Bottom 3 Selling Products (Month – Wise).
4. Salesman With The Number Of  Products They sold (month –wise) ,Top 3 & Bottom 3 Sales Person (month-Wise).



## Process
- Verify data for any missing values and anomalies, and sort out the same.
- Made sure data is consistent and clean with respect to data type, data format and values used.
- Created pivot tables according to the questions asked.
- Merge all pivot tables into one dashboard and apply slicer to make dynamic.


## Project Insight
1. In Year 2021 , The Total Revenue (From Every Product) Was 17.8 lakh Rupees And The Total Product Sales Was 366 Products.

2. In 2021 , The Top 3 Products Were Printer (455 Units Sold) , Mouse (319 Units ) & Monitor (251 Units). On The Other Hand Bottom 3 Products Were Speakers (218 units ) , Scanners (237 units) & Keyboards ( 132 Units ) .

3. Feb & Sept Month’s Recorded The Highest Monthly Revenue (2.24 Lakh & 2.23 lakh Resp.) In 2021 .

4. Mar & Nov Recorded The Lowest Monthly Revenue (86 Thousand  & 31.5 Thousand Rupees).

5. East Region Has Generated The Highest Yearly revenue (7.36 Lakh Rupees ) In 2021.

6. The Top 3 Salesman Were Sidhhu (419 Products Sold) , Rohit Das (335 Products ), Ajit Kumar (394 products). On The Other Hand , The Bottom 3 Sales Person Were Ramesh (144 Products) , Chandu (144 Products) , Amit (146 Products) .



## Conclusion & Next Plan:
- In Order To Grow The Business , We Should Focus on improving sales of low-performing products like Speakers, Scanners, and Keyboards.
- Should Increase marketing efforts in low-revenue months such as March and November.
- Should Maintain and reward top-performing salesmen like Sidhhu and Ajit Kumar to keep them motivated.
- Should Have To Analyze why other regions underperformed compared to the East Region and plan strategies to boost them.
